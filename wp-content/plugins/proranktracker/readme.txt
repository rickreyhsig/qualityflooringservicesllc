== Installation ==

1. Log in to your ProRankTracker's account or sign up on https://proranktracker.com/plans
2. Go to https://proranktracker.com/tools/apps_n_plugins and get your *Token* from the Wordpress box 
3. Log into your WordPress admin panel and go to the Plugins menu. 
4. Search or upload the plugin and activate it.
5. Go to Tools -> ProRankTracker.
6. Enter your ProRankTracker Token and save. 
That’s it!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==


**How can I sign up for a ProRankTracker account?**

Go to: https://proranktracker.com/plans and select the plan that suits your needs.

**Where can I find the Wordpress plugin token on ProRankTracker?**

Log in and go to https://proranktracker.com/tools/apps_n_plugins to get your Token from the Wordpress box.

**Whice URLs will appear in the report displayed on Wordpress?**

The root domain and any deep URL that your wordpress is installed on and match your URLs entered on your ProRankTracker's account.
